Motivation

Prosody began as a personal project to assist my impaired speech (stuttering) in public speaking.
Inspired by a 2015 paper from Wieland et al., which finds evidence that stuttering children have worse rhythm discrimination (i.e. finding a beat) than non-stuttering children, and prior research suggesting that talking to the rhythm of a metronome improves speech flow, I decided to attach a metronome to my wrist to see if it improved my speech.
