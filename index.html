Who am I?

I'm Sebastian. "Sebas" (SEH-bahs) is a nickname my family and friends gave me back in Perú. I'm Peruvian-Colombian.

I'm a third-year doctoral student at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. I search for limitations of human cognitive performance through interface usage for tasks.

I'm currently involved with the Army Research Laboratory on various projects related to Human Factors and interface testing. My doctoral degree is in progress under the guidance of Prof. Alex Kirlik.

In my downtime, you can find me dancing salsa, playing percussion, language-agnostic programming, or enjoying a videogame.
