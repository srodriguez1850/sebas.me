Who am I?

I'm Sebastian. "Sebas" (SEH-bahs) is a nickname my family and friends gave me back in Perú. I'm Peruvian-Colombian.

I'm a second-year doctoral student at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. I search for new and innovative ways to physically interact with technology and extend natural human capabilities with tangible designs.

I currently work at the Beckman Institute for Advanced Science and Technology implementing an interface that facilitates coexistence between autonomous drones and the elderly (dubbed the ASPIRE project), under the guidance of Prof. Alex Kirlik.

In my downtime, you can find me dancing salsa, playing percussion, language-agnostic programming, or enjoying a videogame.
